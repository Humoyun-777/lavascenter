Lavash Center — The Heart of Gulistan

Lavash Center is located in the heart of Gulistan city, in the streets full of hustle and bustle from dawn till late night. It's no exaggeration to say there is no one who doesn't know this place. Every day, the fragrant smell of freshly baked lavash, warm bread, and grilled meat draws in everyone who passes by.

Even on hot summer days and cold snowy winter nights, the doors of Lavash Center never close. The staff, young men who greet you with sincere smiles and sweet-spoken girls—create the true spirit of this place. Our employees are always ready to serve you!

Inside, comfort and well-being prevail. The shining metal trays on every table, the clarity of freshly squeezed juice, the modern music playing in the background—all encourage the guest to stay longer, to relax over a cup of tea. Our place is the best spot for family lunches and friendly gatherings.

But the biggest charm of Lavash Center is, of course, their lavash, incomparable like a queen. Sweet sauce, fragrant spices, and warm meat wrapped in crispy bread—all of this provides a supreme taste with every bite. Some call this lavash "The best taste of Gulistan," while others recall "childhood memories."

In the evening, this place becomes even livelier. Youth, families, workers, and students flock here. Some sit and charge their phones, some sit laughing and joking with friends, and some just grab their lavash and continue on their way.

But everyone leaves with the same feeling—a sense of warmth and fullness. Lavash Center is not just an eatery; it has become a center of sincerity beating in the heart of Gulistan.

It is as if every lavash here is a story, and every guest becomes the hero of that story. We invite you to be a part of this delicious story!
